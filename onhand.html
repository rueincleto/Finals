ENJOY YOUR SUMMER

As a CS student, summer offers balance for me; helping me step away from screens to recharge, reflect, and grow. It helps foster creativity, human connection, and perspective, fueling both personal well-being and future innovation. It's important that just as how hard we work towards achieving our goals may we never forget to enjoy the fruits of our labor. Time is fleeting, our future have a lot of uncertainties and only thing certain in life are changes. Let us enjoy moments, before moments turn into memories.
